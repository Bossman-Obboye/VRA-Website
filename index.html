Welcome to VRA

The Volta River Authority (VRA) is Ghana's leading power generator. We supply related services in a reliable, and safe manner to add financial and social values to our customers and our stakeholders.

Our Mission

We exist to power economies and provide related services; in a safe, reliable and environmentally friendly manner, to add value and raise the living standards of the people of Ghana and West Africa.
